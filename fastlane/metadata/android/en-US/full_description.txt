Agentra is a multi-Agent autonomous discussion and decision-making app. Six professional AI agents — Analyst, Risk Officer, Strategist, Intel Agent, Executor, and Supervisor — conduct structured investment committee meetings around your chosen topics.

Key Features:
- Six autonomous AI Agents: Each agent has its own professional perspective and decision logic
- Multi-round debate and voting: Agents challenge, supplement, and counter each other, producing weighted vote ratings
- Multiple LLM support: Anthropic Claude, DeepSeek, Moonshot (Kimi), GLM (Zhipu)
- Full meeting records: Complete discussion process, vote results, and final ratings are saved
- Smart search integration: Optional web search lets agents access the latest market information
- Self-evolution: Agents conduct post-meeting self-reflection to continuously improve decision quality
- Blackboard collaboration: Agents exchange information through a shared blackboard system
- Sparse activation: Intelligently selects the most relevant agents for each discussion

Runs entirely on your device with data stored locally. Just configure your own LLM API keys to get started.

Perfect for investment research, decision analysis, and developers/researchers interested in Multi-Agent systems.
